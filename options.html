Chrome Scrobbler

 

Enter yout last.fm username and password, and then press save.

To permanently delete your saved username/password, press the delete button.

About

Last.fm Scrobbler for Chrome was created for people who listen to music online through their browser, and would like to keep an updated playback history using last.fm's scrobbling service.

By now the only supported website is YouTube

As I don't visit many other websites with video content, the development of connectors for other websites will be mostly up to you. Feel free to send a pull request at GitHub.


The core of this extension is based on mortenfriis's work which unfortunately doesn't seem to be working right now and original author is not responding to commits or even worse - the website code updates, making the extension useless.
